## Introduction

Our system is a platform designed to simplify the life of the hotel employes. Whether they're a new user or an experienced one, this documentation will guide them through the various aspects of the system.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

To access our system, simply [yse the setup.exe]. 
### Logging In

If you already have an account, click on the "Login" button and enter your credentials.

## Navigation

The system is organized into the following main sections:

- Statistics
- Booking
- Rooms
- Restauration
- Staffs
- Event plans


Use the navigation menu on the left to switch between these sections.

## Features and Functionality

### Offline feature

When the system is offline you can use it and make the reservation for the clients and when the connection it's restored , the data will be posted to the database

### Statistics

The statistics page provides all the statistics of the hotel and its only for the hotel manager.

### Booking

the booking page, its escpicially for the reception team 
### Rooms

The Rooms page displays the rooms if they'r available or not and who is in the room

### Restauration

## Staffs
